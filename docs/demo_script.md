## Q&A Preparation

### Anticipated Questions and Responses

**Q: How do you ensure clinical accuracy?**
A: Our AI is trained on clinical datasets and continuously validated against gold standards. Licensed therapists review all protocols, and our compliance agent monitors every interaction for safety.

**Q: What about data privacy with children's information?**
A: We're fully HIPAA compliant with end-to-end encryption. Data is anonymized for AI training, and parents have complete control over their child's information with easy export and deletion options.

**Q: How does this compare to human therapists?**
A: We enhance, not replace, human expertise. Our platform increases therapist productivity 3x while providing children with daily practice opportunities that weren't possible before.

**Q: What's your go-to-market strategy?**
A: We're partnering with healthcare systems, schools, and insurance providers. Our B2B2C model ensures clinical oversight while making therapy accessible to families.

**Q: How do you handle different types of speech disorders?**
A: Currently focused on articulation disorders in autistic children, but our platform is designed to expand to other communication challenges as we gather more data and clinical validation.

**Q: What's your competitive moat?**
A: Our multi-agent AI architecture, clinical safety systems, and deep autism specialization create significant barriers to entry. Plus, our data advantage grows stronger with each child we serve.
